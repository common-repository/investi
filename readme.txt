Share price charts and announcement widgets for the ASX, LSE, AIM & FSE. 

== Description ==

Share price charts and announcement widgets for the ASX, LSE, AIM & FSE. Our comprehensive and timely data quickly summarises your current and historical share price performance,displaying charts and widgets to enhance engagement with your investor audience.

== Installation ==

Install Investi either via the WordPress.org plugin directory or by uploading the files to your server.

After activating Investi, go to the plugin settings page by clicking on 'Investi' in the admin menu

== Third Party Services

The charting library is provided by [highcharts](https://www.highcharts.com/) - their privacy policy is available [here](https://www.highcharts.com/privacy)
